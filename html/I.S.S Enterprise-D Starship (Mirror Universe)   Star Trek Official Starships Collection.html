Description -

The wait is finally over! This superb bonus edition of the I.S.S Enterprise-D Starship was created by the Terran Empire as a Galaxy x-class attack cruiser. This starship has travelled through the mirror universe and now has landed exclusively into the Star Trek Official Starships Collection!

The impressive design of the I.S.S.Enterprise-D Starship, with its distinctive third nacelle, is based upon the future U.S.S. Enterprise seen in the Next Generation episode, ‘All Good Things’. Author Dayton Ward takes a look at the many Star Trek adventures to take place in the Mirror Universe in novels and comic books.

This starship comes packed with in-depth information about your starship and the epic adventures of the mirror universe!

What are you waiting for boldly going where TNG has not gone before.
