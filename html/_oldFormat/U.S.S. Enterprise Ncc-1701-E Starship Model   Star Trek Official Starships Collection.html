Description -

Welcome one of the most eagerly awaited models in the Star Trek Official Starship Collection, the U.S.S Enterprise NCC-1701-E.

This Enterprise was created for Star Trek: First Contact and was designed to be a tougher, sleeker and more advanced ship than her predecessor. Every time this Enterprise appeared there were subtle differences and the die-cast model is based on the CG version of the ship that was created for Star Trek: Insurrection.

The accompanying magazine takes a look at John Eaves original design concepts and the complicated history of the physical and CG models that were used onscreen.
