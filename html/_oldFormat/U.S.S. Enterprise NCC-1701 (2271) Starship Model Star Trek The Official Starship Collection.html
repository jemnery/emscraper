Issue 2

£12.99

Email me when available

Description -

Issue 2 brings you the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 as she appeared in STAR TREK: THE MOTION PICTURE after the refit that followed Captain Kirk’s original five-year mission. Will make a welcome addition to your Star Trek The Official Starship Collection as you imagine boldly exploring where no man has gone before! 

This detailed U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 (2271) Starship Model has been created in die-cast and painted using references from CBS Studios own archives of designs, physical and CG models.

Accompanied by a special collector’s magazine that provides an in-depth profile of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 ship and exclusive behind the scenes articles reveal how the ship was designed, complete with original design sketches.
