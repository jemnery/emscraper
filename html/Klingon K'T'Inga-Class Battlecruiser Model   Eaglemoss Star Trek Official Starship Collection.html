Description -

The model ship featured in the latest edition of Star Trek Official Starship Collection is the Klingon K'T'Inga-Class Battlecruiser, based on the Star Trek: The Motion Picture version.

Like the Enterprise, the Klingon ship got a significant upgrade for its appearance in the first Star Trek movie, with new design work done by Richard Taylor and Andy Probert. This version has much more surface detail than its original series predecessor but has a more straightforward, gunmetal paint scheme than the CG version that appeared in Deep Space Nine over 20 years later.

The accompanying magazine provides an inside look at the design process, with some rare photographs of the studio model and a profile of the ship.
