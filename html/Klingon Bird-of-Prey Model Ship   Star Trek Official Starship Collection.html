Klingon Bird-Of-Prey Model - Issue 3

Issue 3

Description -

Issue 3 of the Star Trek Official Starship Collection delivers Commander Kruge’s Klingon Bird-of-Prey, the heavily armed stealth ship part responsible for the destruction of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701, before it was seized by Captain Kirk’s surviving crew.

The classic Klingon Bird-of-Prey, which made its debut in STAR TREK III: THE SEARCH FOR SPOCK, and would become known as the ultimate raiding and fighting ship that was used by the Klingon Empire for hundreds of years.

The magazine looks into the origination of this now familiar Klingon vessel for Star Trek III: The Search for Spock, following initial plans that had the Romulans pegged as the movie  s main enemies.
